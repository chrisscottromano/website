Work

I also work on HCI and human factors for SenseWhy, Inc.
I occasionally consult on mobile health technology for Behavioralize LLC.


School

I attended Loyola University Chicago, where I served a term as student council president, published poems in the campus fine arts magazine, worked off-campus as an audio engineer, studied abroad in Madrid, and graduated with a BS in Psychology.


Life

I have lived in Chicago, IL since 2015. I like making music and writing comedy, and I am slowly working towards my goal of creating a video game. I read a lot of news.

Interests

I am interested in developing body sensor networks that infer behavioral and psychological constructs from biophysical processes. For instance, Dr. Mahdi Pedram and I are designing a wearable keyword spotter that tallies absolutist words and total words spoken by the wearer, returning the ratio between them. This ratio is greater in patients with affective disorders. Thus, our device could serve as a diagnostic tool or as a way to guage the efficacy of an ongoing psychiatric treatment.
