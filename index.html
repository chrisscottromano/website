Work

I am a researcher working on wearable sensing for behavior medicine at the HABits Lab.
I also work on HCI and human factors for SenseWhy, Inc.
I occasionally consult on mobile health technology for Behavioralize LLC.
I work with AIRO, a personal AI startup that was featured at CES 2024.
I write satire and humor pieces for various publications (see humor tab).


Life

I have lived in Chicago, IL since 2015. I like writing, making music, and building offbeat things.
